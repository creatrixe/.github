## What we believe

The cultural moment is loud about AI <em>replacing</em> humans. We believe the AI worth paying for is the kind that <em>relieves</em> humans of the work that benefited least from their attention — handing back the time that craft requires.

That belief is the order of operations:

- **Domain expertise first.** Depth in the work itself — restaurants, plumbing, clinics, salons, mid-market ops — earned by watching the work for years, not by reading a Wikipedia page yesterday.
- **AI second.** As the executor of what humans have crafted with care. Around the clock, identically every time. Not as the centre of gravity with humans filling its gaps.
- **Pricing is downstream.** We charge a fraction of the value we deliver and stay with clients while it compounds. [Read the perspective →](https://creatrixe.com/blog/human-assisted-ai-vs-ai-assisted-workflows/)
